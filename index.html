Simon

“ Simon ” is a captivating white sculpture that embodies the complex layers of a person's identity and emotions. The sculpture features a human face that has been meticulously crafted into horizontal layers, with each layer elegantly twisted in the opposite direction. This unique design symbolizes the intricate and ever-evolving nature of the human psyche.

10 inches in height, 6 inches in width, and 6 inches in depth. This compact size allows it to be displayed on a tabletop, shelf, or any other preferred location, making it a versatile and intriguing piece of art.

Measuring just 12 inches tall and 6 inches wide, this sculpture is the perfect size for a side table, bookshelf, or mantel. It is also lightweight and easy to move, so you can display it anywhere in your home or office.

“ Reflect ” is a thought-provoking 3D-printed sculpture that presents a man's face, precisely cut in 3/4 profile, with his gaze turned inwards towards the rest of his face. This sculpture symbolizes self-reflection and the introspective journey of personal growth.

The size of the sculpture is modest, making it suitable for display on a side table, desk, or in an office setting. Its sleek design and clean lines are both modern and elegant, ensuring it complements various decor styles.

Cherie's Elegance" is a petite expressionist acrylic painting. It measures 12 inches by 16 inches, making it an intimate piece that conveys the vibrant and colorful essence of the subject, Cherie.
